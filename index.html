Course Information

This course aims to present an in-depth introduction to the field of continuous optimization. Course materials cover optimality conditions, gradient method, Newton’s method, convex sets and convex functions, duality theory, and advanced firstorder algorithms. Applications in machine learning, statistics, signal processing etc. will also be discussed.

Textbooks

Reference Books:

  • Introduction to Nonlinear Optimization: Theory, Algorithms, and Applications with MATLAB , by Amir Beck (electronic copy available online)
  • Convex Optimization, by S. Boyd and L. Vandenberghe (electronic copy available online)
  • Nonlinear Programming, by D. Bertsekas
  • First-Order Methods in Optimization, by Amir Beck (electronic copy available online)
